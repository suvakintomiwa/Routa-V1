About / Mission

Routa empowers local couriers and micro-entrepreneurs with transparent, peer-to-peer logistics. Where payments flow instantly, and governance belongs to the community.

Ecosystem

The Routa ecosystem connects riders, users, and local businesses through blockchain rails — creating trustless delivery, open coordination, and shared upside.

Roadmap

Phase 1

Pilot launch in South West Nigeria. Onboard couriers and run early settlement tests.

Phase 2

Base integration, crypto/fiat payments, in-app wallet.

Phase 3

Governance activation, reward pools, and regional scaling.
